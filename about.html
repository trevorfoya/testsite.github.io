We are IHD Care

An organisation dedicated to helping adults experiencing long-term mental health difficulties live with dignity, independence and hope.

Who we are

Our mission & ethos

IHD Care offers adults with various mental health difficulties a family-setting residential experience where each individual is respected, valued and nurtured to allow their talents and strengths to develop.

We encourage residents to develop their confidence and wellbeing, leading to fulfilment of their independence. Our staff aim to foster positive relationships through shared daily life in a well-maintained, safe and stimulating environment.

We work closely with all agencies to enable residents to reintegrate back into communities, families or independent living. Care plans are actively reviewed and monitored, and feedback is always welcomed.

We work with adults experiencing: long and enduring mental health difficulties, substance misuse and other complex needs. Our programme provides a step-down service, helping individuals move from supported living to fully independent living.
