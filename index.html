About IHD Care

A family-centred approach to supported living

We offer adults with mental health difficulties a family setting where each individual is respected, valued and nurtured to develop their strengths.

Person-centred care planning

Bespoke support plans designed with you, your family and your care team.

Partnership with CMHT & Social Services

Working closely with Community Mental Health Teams, GPs, Social Workers and local authorities.

Three-tier move-on programme

High, medium and floating support — helping every resident progress towards independence.

How it works

Our referral process

1
Submit a referral
Complete our online form with background history, care plan and risk assessment.
2
Joint assessment
We arrange a joint assessment with you and the relevant professionals.
3
Home visit
The service user visits their proposed home to meet staff and other residents.
4
Agree & move in
Once funding is agreed, we arrange a smooth, supported transition.
