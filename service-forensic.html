Forensic Placements & Support

Specialist 24-hour supported living for individuals with a forensic history, focused on safe community reintegration.

Specialist forensic support

IHD Care offers a 24-hour supported living service and a supportive environment that focuses on the individual needs and aspirations of the people we support. Our supported living pathways enable us to work towards increasing independence, minimising risk and reducing support over time — with the ultimate goal of more independent living.

Who we work with

We offer high-quality supported living for people with a forensic history, particularly those on Section 37/41. We understand the complexities and legalities of community-based support for individuals who have been discharged from secure settings or who have offended in the past.

  • People discharged from secure psychiatric units
  • Individuals on Section 37/41 requiring structured community support
  • Those with a history of offending who are ready for community living
  • People requiring a least-restrictive alternative to secure care

Our approach

Our team is focused on promoting independence while identifying and managing risks — resulting in a community-based support model that provides a "least restrictive" alternative to secure care.

Our experienced staff understand that many people with a forensic history have developed a deep mistrust of health and social care professionals. We have the experience, resilience and passion required to work through these challenges, break down barriers and form productive, positive relationships.

Working with multidisciplinary teams

We work very closely with the Ministry of Justice, probation services, CMHT, Responsible Clinicians and Social Supervisors to ensure all legal requirements are met and that risk is managed effectively throughout the placement.
