Intensive Support

Specialist 2:1 and 1:1 support packages, 24 hours a day, for service users with challenging or complex needs.

What is intensive support?

Our intensive support service provides high-level, around-the-clock care for service users who have particularly challenging needs or behaviours in the community. These packages are carefully designed in collaboration with multidisciplinary teams to ensure the right level of support in the right environment.

Support packages available

  • 2:1 support — two support workers to one service user, 24 hours a day
  • Independent accommodation with 1:1 staff, 24 hours per day
  • A small 4-bed group home with staff on-site 24 hours per day

How we work

Service users are closely monitored and we work in close partnership with multidisciplinary teams to ensure anyone on a Community Treatment Order (CTO) or with a forensic history is managed appropriately and safely.

Crucially, we provide rehabilitation in the individual's chosen home destination — eliminating the stress of repeated moves and allowing genuine attachment and progress.

Eligibility

This service is suitable for adults whose needs cannot be safely met by a standard supported living placement. Referrals are accepted from CMHT, Social Services, secure settings and hospital discharge teams.
