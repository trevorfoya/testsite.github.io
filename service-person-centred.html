Person Centred Solutions

Bespoke support plans co-designed with service users, families and professionals to achieve the best outcomes.

Care built around the individual

We offer person-centred solutions to every service user referred to us. This means involving other professionals, families and — most importantly — the service user themselves in designing support plans that genuinely reflect their goals, preferences and needs.

The range we cover

  • Intensive 1:1 support
  • High needs supported living
  • Medium needs supported living
  • Low needs supported living
  • Floating support
  • Respite care
  • Managing personal budgets
  • Learning and development support
  • Employment services
  • Crisis support

How it works

There is a great variety of supported housing and residential care available, and different types of accommodation suit different levels of need. Our three-tier support programme allows service users to gain confidence and develop independent skills, while providing continuity of care throughout every stage of that journey.

We never impose a one-size-fits-all approach. Your care plan is yours.
