Referring a Client to IHD Care

We accept referrals from CMHT, Social Services, hospitals and GPs. Our referral process is straightforward and supportive.

How to refer a client

We work closely with Community Mental Health Teams and other professionals working in local hospitals with specialised units, to facilitate a smooth discharge into our service. We welcome referrals from a wide range of professional sources.

Referral criteria

To process a referral, we will need:

  • Background history of the service user
  • Current care plan
  • Current risk assessment

Our referral process

Once we receive the above information, we will arrange a joint assessment of the service user's needs with the referrer and relevant professionals. If the service user meets our criteria, they will be invited to visit the proposed home, meet staff and other residents, and view their accommodation.

Once the service user has agreed to move in, and we have agreed costings and funding with Social Services, we will arrange their move accordingly — ensuring the transition is as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Who we accept referrals from

  • Community Mental Health Teams (CMHT)
  • NHS hospital discharge teams and specialised psychiatric units
  • Local Authority Social Services
  • General Practitioners (GPs)
  • Probation and Ministry of Justice (for forensic placements)
  • Families and private individuals
